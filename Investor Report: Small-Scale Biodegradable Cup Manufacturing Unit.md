## 1. Executive Summary

This report presents a compelling investment opportunity in the rapidly growing biodegradable packaging market in India. We propose the establishment of a small-scale manufacturing unit for biodegradable cups with an initial investment of ₹7-10 lakhs. The business is poised to capitalize on the increasing demand for sustainable alternatives to plastic, driven by government regulations and a shift in consumer preferences. The venture is projected to be highly profitable, with a potential Return on Investment (ROI) of over 160% in the first year of operations.

## 2. Market Opportunity

The Indian market for biodegradable packaging is expanding at a significant rate. The paper cup segment, in particular, presents a lucrative opportunity for new entrants.

*   **Market Growth:** The India paper cups market is projected to grow from 23.2 billion units in 2024 to 29.0 billion units by 2033, demonstrating a steady demand [1].
*   **Regulatory Tailwinds:** The nationwide ban on single-use plastics has created a substantial market gap that biodegradable products are well-positioned to fill [2].
*   **Consumer Demand:** There is a growing consciousness among Indian consumers about the environmental impact of plastic waste, leading to a preference for eco-friendly products.

## 3. Business Proposal

We propose a lean and efficient business model focused on the manufacturing and B2B supply of biodegradable paper and bagasse cups.

*   **Products:** High-quality, customizable biodegradable cups for hot and cold beverages.
*   **Target Market:** Cafes, restaurants, quick-service restaurants (QSRs), corporate offices, and event organizers.
*   **Unique Selling Proposition (USP):** A combination of superior product quality, competitive pricing, customization options, and a strong commitment to sustainability.
