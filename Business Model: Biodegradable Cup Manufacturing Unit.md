# Business Model: Biodegradable Cup Manufacturing Unit

**Investment Range:** ₹7-10 Lakhs

**Author:** Manus AI

**Date:** November 06, 2025

## 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines a business model for establishing a small-scale biodegradable cup manufacturing unit in India with an initial investment of ₹7-10 lakhs. The business will focus on producing high-quality, eco-friendly paper and bagasse-based cups to cater to the growing demand for sustainable packaging solutions. The model is designed to be profitable and scalable, leveraging the favorable market conditions created by the Indian government's ban on single-use plastics and increasing consumer awareness.

## 2. Business Description

### 2.1. Mission and Vision

*   **Mission:** To manufacture and supply affordable, high-quality biodegradable cups, contributing to a cleaner environment and providing a sustainable alternative to plastic.
*   **Vision:** To become a leading supplier of biodegradable tableware in the local and regional market, known for product quality, innovation, and commitment to sustainability.

### 2.2. Business Structure

Given the projected turnover and the need for future scalability, a **Private Limited Company** or **Proprietorship** is the recommended legal structure. This will allow for equity funding, provide limited liability protection (in case of Pvt Ltd), and facilitate business growth [1].

## 3. Products and Services

The primary products will be biodegradable cups made from paper and other eco-friendly materials like sugarcane bagasse. The product line will include:

*   **Paper Cups:** Single-wall and double-wall cups for hot and cold beverages.
*   **Bagasse Cups:** 100% compostable cups made from sugarcane waste.
*   **Custom Printing:** Offering branded cups for businesses, cafes, and events.

## 4. Market Analysis

The Indian biodegradable packaging market is experiencing significant growth, with the paper cups segment being a major contributor. The market is driven by a combination of regulatory push and consumer pull. Key market highlights include:

*   **Market Size:** The India paper cups market reached 23.2 billion units in 2024 and is projected to grow to 29.0 billion units by 2033 [2].
*   **Growth Drivers:** The nationwide ban on single-use plastics, rising environmental consciousness, and the expansion of the food and beverage industry are key growth drivers [3].
*   **Target Market:** The primary target market will be B2B customers, including cafes, restaurants, corporate offices, and event organizers in the local and regional area.

## 5. Operations Plan (₹7-10 Lakh Investment)

### 5.1. Manufacturing Process

The manufacturing process involves several stages, from raw material processing to finished product packaging. The process is relatively straightforward and can be managed by a small team.

### 5.2. Machinery and Equipment

### 5.3. Raw Materials

The primary raw materials required are:

*   **Paper Board:** PE-coated or PLA-coated paper rolls.
*   **Bottom Reel:** For the base of the cups.
*   **Inks:** Food-grade printing inks.
*   **Packaging Material:** For packing the finished cups.

An initial investment of **₹1-2 lakhs** will be required for raw material procurement.

## 6. Marketing and Sales Strategy

### 6.1. Target Audience

*   **Primary:** Cafes, restaurants, QSRs, and corporate offices in the local city and surrounding areas.
*   **Secondary:** Event management companies, caterers, and wholesalers.

### 6.2. Marketing Channels

*   **Direct Sales:** A dedicated sales executive will approach potential B2B clients.
*   **Digital Marketing:** A simple website and social media presence to showcase products and generate leads.
*   **Local Listings:** Listing the business on platforms like IndiaMART, Justdial, and local business directories.

## 7. Management Team

For a small-scale unit, a lean team is recommended:

*   **Production Manager/Owner:** Oversees overall operations, sales, and finances.
*   **Machine Operator (Skilled):** Operates and maintains the paper cup machine.
*   **Helper (Unskilled):** Assists in raw material handling, packing, and dispatch.
