# Investment and Financial Data - Paper Cup Manufacturing

## Source: IndiaFilings

### Investment Requirements

**Minimum Investment:** ₹10 lakhs
- Equipment and raw materials purchase
- Production capacity: Over 2.2 crore (22 million) paper cups
- Estimated sales turnover: Over ₹66 lakhs

### Bank Loan Structure
- **Term Loan:** ₹10 lakhs
- **Working Capital:** ₹15 lakhs
- **Total Loan Required:** ₹25 lakhs
- **Loan Scheme:** MUDRA loan scheme available
- **Loan amount:** Up to ₹10 lakhs without collateral

### Business Structure
- Recommended: Private Limited Company
- Reason: Turnover exceeds ₹66 lakhs per annum
- Benefits: Easy bank loan access, ability to scale-up, bring in partners

### Required Registrations
1. Company Registration (Private Limited recommended)
2. GST Registration (for selling paper cups)
3. Additional licenses as needed

### Land and Building
- Required area: Approximately 500 square meters
- Requirement: Electricity connection

### Equipment/Machinery
- **Automatic paper cup manufacturing machine:** ₹8,50,000
- **Printing and dyes:** ₹1,50,000
- **Total Machinery Cost:** ₹10,00,000

### Labour Requirement
- **Minimal requirement:** About 3 persons
  - 1 Sales cum Production Manager
  - 1 Skilled worker
  - 1 Unskilled worker

### Raw Materials
- Printed PE paper
- Bottom reel
- Packing material
- **Monthly investment in raw materials:** ₹4 lakhs

### Profitability Analysis

**Investment Breakdown:**
- Machinery: ₹10 lakhs
- Working Capital: ₹15 lakhs
- **Total Investment:** ₹25 lakhs

**Financial Projections:**
- **Annual Turnover:** ₹66 lakhs
- **Total Production Cost:** ₹57 lakhs

- **Net Profit Ratio:** 14%
